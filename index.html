About Me

My name is Yasir Mohamud. I am a software developer who specializes in Javascript, HTML, and CSS. A bit about my background, I worked for the Seattle great wheel for my first job and worked there for almost 3 years. During my time there I was promoted to Operator of the Ferris wheel and would take on a lot of responsibilities such as managing staff breaks, lunches and making sure operations were going smooth which gave me some leadership skills. I then quit and went to a code Bootcamp to pursue my passion for coding. I achieved and acquired many skills and experiences such as critical thinking, self-learning, and many others. I also got to hone my skills and strengthen my weaknesses. I am in love with coding and I would like to encourage others who have an interest in software development and be not only a mentor but also a symbol of hard work pays off.
